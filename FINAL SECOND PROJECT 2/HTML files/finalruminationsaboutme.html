ASHA ADISA

Asha Adisa is a 31 year old musician and music historian based in Chicago, IL. A former Howard University music student, Asha channels their knowledge of Black American musical traditions and fuses it with their obsession with esoteric expansion. Their vast experiences at the intersections of blackness, queerness and trans identity facilitate an equally exploratory and enthralling sound. Their passion for music is only paralleled by a love of nurturing people and their unique gifts.
